# Code of Conduct

Contributors must communicate respectfully, critique work rather than people, and provide evidence for factual or legal claims.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory abuse, deliberate dataset poisoning, fabricated provenance, and knowingly submitting unauthorised text are not accepted.

Maintainers may edit, reject, lock, or remove contributions that violate these expectations. Serious or repeated misconduct may result in exclusion from the project.

Raise conduct concerns privately with the repository owner when public discussion would expose personal information or worsen harm.
