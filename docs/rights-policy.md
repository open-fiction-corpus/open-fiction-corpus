# Rights policy

A work is eligible for public release only when its redistribution basis is documented per edition.

Review the author and every rights-relevant contributor, including translators and editors. Record the jurisdictions actually checked rather than claiming worldwide public-domain status.

`uncertain` works may remain as research candidates in the catalogue but cannot enter public release packs. A source project's inclusion decision is useful evidence, not a substitute for this project's review.

Contemporary author submissions require explicit permission or an open licence that covers redistribution of the text and the intended machine-learning use. The submitter must be able to grant those rights.

When evidence conflicts or remains incomplete, exclude the work from release until resolved. This project records research and provenance; it does not issue legal opinions.
